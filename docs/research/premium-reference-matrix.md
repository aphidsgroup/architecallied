## Cross-cutting conclusions for the three directions
- Scale contrast (≥10:1 display:body) is the single strongest premium signal available without photography → all directions.
- Corner/edge-anchored micro-typography turns empty space into composed space → A, C heroes.
- Index/archive framing dignifies the absence of published projects ("archive in preparation" vs "0 projects") → B, and the production projects page after selection.
- The datum must become a system: nav indicator → section transition → index connector → footer signature, not one decorative line.
- Footer as designed conclusion, not link dump → all directions.
